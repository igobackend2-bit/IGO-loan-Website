Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026 · IGO Loans & Subsidy

At IGO Loans & Subsidy, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the highest level of security for your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary to provide our consultancy services, including but not limited to:

  • Identity Information: Full name, date of birth, gender, and photograph.
  • Contact Information: Mobile number, email address, permanent and communication address.
  • KYC Documents: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, and other government-issued identification.
  • Financial Information: Bank statements, income tax returns (ITR), land records, and project reports.
  • Business Information: MSME/Udyam registration details, company incorporation documents, and GST details.

2. Purpose of Collection

  • Assessing eligibility for various government subsidies and bank loan schemes.
  • Preparing project reports and application dossiers for submission to financial institutions.
  • Facilitating communication between you and banks or government agencies (e.g., NABARD, MSME Ministry).
  • Compliance with anti-money laundering (AML) and KYC regulations.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with authorized banking partners, government departments, technical partners, and legal authorities as required by law.

4. Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data against unauthorized access. Our systems use industry-standard encryption and follow the IT Act, 2000 and the DPDP Act, 2023 guidelines.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by regulatory requirements. Post-fulfillment, data is securely archived or deleted.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and review the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time (subject to contractual obligations).
  • Request deletion of your data once the service purpose is fulfilled.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage periodic review.
