Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At IGO Loans & Subsidy (a unit of IGO Group), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the highest level of security for your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the data you provide to us in connection with our financial consultancy services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary to provide our consultancy services, including but not limited to:

  • Identity Information: Full name, date of birth, gender, and photograph.
  • Contact Information: Mobile number, email address, permanent and communication address.
  • KYC Documents: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, and other government-issued identification.
  • Financial Information: Bank statements, income tax returns (ITR), land records, and project reports.
  • Business Information: MSME/Udyam registration details, company incorporation documents, and GST details.

2. Purpose of Collection

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Assessing eligibility for various government subsidies and bank loan schemes.
  • Preparing project reports and application dossiers for submission to financial institutions.
  • Facilitating communication between you and banks or government agencies (e.g., NABARD, MSME Ministry).
  • Compliance with anti-money laundering (AML) and "Know Your Customer" (KYC) regulations.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share your information with:

  • Scheduled Commercial Banks & NBFCs: For the purpose of processing your loan applications.
  • Government Departments: For the purpose of securing subsidies and infrastructure grants.
  • Technical Partners: Professionals such as Chartered Accountants or Engineers involved in project report preparation.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Our systems use industry-standard encryption and follow the guidelines set forth by the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP), 2023.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by regulatory requirements. Post-fulfillment, data is archived or securely deleted as per our internal retention policy.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time (subject to contractual or legal obligations).
  • Request the deletion of your data once the service purpose is fulfilled.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
