Our Loan & Subsidy Services

Comprehensive financial assistance tailored for Tamil Nadu's farmers, rural communities, and small businesses.

🌾

Agricultural Loans

Short-term and long-term crop loans for paddy, sugarcane, pulses, and horticulture. We assist with bank linkage, documentation, and disbursement follow-up.

Up to ₹50 Lakh
💳

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Get quick access to revolving credit for seeds, fertilizers, and other farming needs with subsidized interest rates under the government KCC scheme.

Interest @ 4% p.a.

MSME / Business Loans

Collateral-free loans for micro and small enterprises under PMMY (Mudra), CGTMSE, and PMEGP schemes. Ideal for artisans, traders, and start-ups.

Upto ₹10 Lakh
