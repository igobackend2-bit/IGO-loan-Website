Subsidy Claim Roadmap

A technical guide to the 5-phase journey from project planning to successful government subsidy credit.

1

Planning & Feasibility

Site selection, soil/water testing, and determining project scope for Polyhouse, Livestock, or Solar. Initial consultation with IGO experts.

2

Technical Documentation

Generation of the 45-page Detailed Project Report (DPR), architectural layouts, and cost estimates aligned with NHB/MIDH guidelines.

3

Bank & LOI Approval

Submission to bank for term loan and obtaining the critical "Letter of Intent" (LOI) from National Horticulture Board (NHB) before construction starts.

4

Construction & Execution

Implementation of the project as per approved specifications. Concurrent application for PM-KUSUM (Solar) and PMFBY (Insurance).

5

Joint Inspection & Credit

Joint site inspection by NHB/MIDH/Bank. Submission of completion certificate and direct subsidy credit to the bank loan account.
