Subsidy Claim Roadmap

Master DPR Guide

A technical guide to the 5-phase journey from project planning to successful government subsidy credit. Follow each phase to maximize your chances of approval.

The 5-Phase Journey

DPR to Subsidy Roadmap

1

Planning & Feasibility

Week 1–2

Site selection, soil/water testing, and determining project scope for Polyhouse, Livestock, or Solar. Initial consultation with IGO experts to select the optimal funding combination.

2

Technical Documentation

Week 2–4

Generation of the 45-page Detailed Project Report (DPR), architectural layouts, and cost estimates aligned with NHB/MIDH guidelines. This document is the backbone of your application.

3

Bank & LOI Approval

Critical Step Week 4–8

Submission to bank for term loan and obtaining the critical "Letter of Intent" (LOI) from National Horticulture Board (NHB) before construction starts. This step is mandatory — construction before LOI forfeits the subsidy.

4

Construction & Execution

Month 2–6

Implementation of the project as per approved specifications. Concurrent application for PM-KUSUM (Solar) and PMFBY (Crop Insurance). Progress photos must be maintained throughout.

5

Joint Inspection & Credit

Month 6–8

Joint site inspection by NHB/MIDH/Bank officials. Submission of completion certificate and verification of compliance. Subsidy is directly credited to the bank loan account within 30–60 days.
